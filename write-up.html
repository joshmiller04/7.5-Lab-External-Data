So I started to ask Copilotwhat. I started by asking simple questions to try to figure out why I was receiving errors and overall the counter was not working. I continued to ask questions relating to my issues and once CoPilot got a better understanding of what I needed it was helpful. Although often when asking CoPilot questions and when it would give me code that did not work it was very frustrating to keep tyring code it would give me but was not what I wanted.
